About Us

The Heart of Ethiopian Coffee Culture

Yegna Coffee was born from a passion for Ethiopia's rich coffee heritage. We work directly with small-scale farmers across Sidama, Yirgacheffe, and Limu regions to bring you the finest beans.

Our coffee is hand-picked, sun-dried, and roasted in small batches to preserve the unique flavors that make Ethiopian coffee world-famous.
